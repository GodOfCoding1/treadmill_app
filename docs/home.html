Features

A focused app for people who want direct control over their treadmill instead of a manufacturer’s locked-down companion app.

Scan & connect

Find nearby treadmills over BLE and connect with the full FTMS setup sequence.

Live dashboard

See speed, incline, distance, time, calories, and heart rate as your treadmill reports them.

Manual control

Adjust speed and incline with sliders bounded to your treadmill’s supported ranges.

Interval builder

Create reorderable workout plans with custom intervals and save them locally.

Workout engine

Run plans automatically with countdowns, progress, and smooth interval transitions.

Activity calendar

Track completed sessions on your phone with optional reminders and streak nudges.

How it works

Three steps from scan to structured workout.

1

Scan

Turn on Bluetooth, open Treadmill, and pick your FTMS-compatible device from the scan list.

2

Build

Create an interval plan with target speeds and inclines, or use manual control from the dashboard.

3

Run

Start your workout and let the app send target changes to the treadmill in real time.
