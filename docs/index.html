This Privacy Policy describes how the Treadmill mobile application (“App”) handles information when you use it to connect to and control FTMS-compatible treadmills over Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE).

Summary

Treadmill is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not operate user accounts, analytics services, or cloud backends for this App. Data you generate while using the App stays on your phone unless you choose to share it yourself (for example, by exporting or screenshotting content).

Information the App Uses

Depending on how you use the App, the following information may be processed:

  • Bluetooth and nearby devices. The App scans for nearby BLE devices to find treadmills, connects to the device you select, and exchanges fitness-machine data (such as speed, incline, distance, elapsed time, heart rate, and energy) with that treadmill using the open FTMS standard.
  • Workout plans. Custom interval plans you create (names, speeds, inclines, and durations) are stored locally on your device.
  • Activity history. Completed sessions that meet the App’s minimum duration threshold may be saved locally, including start/end times, duration, workout type, optional plan name, distance, average speed, calories, and heart rate when reported by the treadmill.
  • Reminder settings. If you enable notifications, your chosen reminder times and on/off preferences are stored locally so the App can schedule local alerts.

Information Stored on Your Device

The App uses your device’s local storage (Android SharedPreferences) to save workout plans, activity history, and reminder settings. This data is not transmitted to RunForge or any third-party server by the App.

Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Bluetooth — required to discover, connect to, and control your treadmill.
  • Location (older Android versions only) — on some Android versions, the operating system requires location permission for BLE scanning even though the App does not use your GPS location for tracking. On Android 12 and above, the App requests Bluetooth scan access without using it for location.
  • Notifications — used only for optional workout reminders and streak nudges that you configure in settings.
  • Foreground service and wake lock — used during active workouts so the BLE connection and workout timer continue while the App is in the background (for example, when the screen is off).
  • Boot completed — used to reschedule local notifications after your device restarts, if you have reminders enabled.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number through the App.
  • We do not sell or rent personal information.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs in the App.
  • We do not upload your workout or health-related data to our servers.

Third-Party Services

The App communicates directly with your treadmill over BLE. It does not send your personal data to treadmill manufacturers through RunForge servers. Your use of Google Play to install or update the App is subject to Google’s own privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will assist with deletion of locally stored data on the device.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data stored by the App remains on your device until you delete it within the App (where supported), clear the App’s storage in Android system settings, or uninstall the App.

Security

Because data stays on your device, its security also depends on your device settings (screen lock, backups, and who has physical access to your phone). BLE communication occurs between your phone and your treadmill within normal Bluetooth range.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes become effective means you accept the updated policy.
